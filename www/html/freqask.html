Frequently Asked Questions


  • What is ReachLink?

    ReachLink is a cutting-edge tele-behavioral health program that offers unique care for mental and behavioral health. One of the ways ReachLink therapists provide therapy is by using video technology that feels similar to Skype or FaceTime to deliver intimate and remote therapy sessions.

  • Can ReachLink substitute for traditional face-to-face therapy?

    Absolutely. Many users find that ReachLink is more clinically effective, accessible, affordable and convenient than traditional, “in-person” therapy. In fact, 91% of health outcomes were as good or better via tele-health, according to americanwell.com

  • Who will be helping me and how are they qualified?

    Therapists are selected for specific ReachLink programs based on their experience in the industry and credentials. All of our therapists have a minimum of a masters-level education in addition to being licensed in the state in which they practice. Our therapists are selected from private practice where they have spent at least 2 years providing care in their respective field. The ReachLink clinical oversight team provides quality assurance for all ReachLink programs.

  • How will I communicate with my counselor?

    Therapy is delivered via the ReachLink video platform. Additionally, you will have access to ReachLink care teams via different channels including email, phone, text and chat.

  • How do live video sessions work?

    Once enrolled in a ReachLink program, users schedule therapy sessions with their therapist. For video sessions, a therapist will send a link to your preferred device that will initiate a video session.

  • How long can I use ReachLink?

    As long as you need. ReachLink recommends a minimum of one, eight-week program. You and your therapist may determine more or less time, as professionally determined, on a case-by-case basis.

  • Is ReachLink right for me?

    Many people continue to experience the benefits of ReachLink programs. Call our client care team or request for a specialist to contact you to find out if you qualify for a ReachLink program.

  • How much does it cost? Is ReachLink covered by insurance?

    ReachLink is covered by most insurances, in many cases including Medicare and Medicaid. We are still in the process of becoming accessible to all 50 states. To find out if ReachLink is covered in your state and insurance, call our client care team or request for a specialist to contact you to find out if your insurance qualifies for a ReachLink program.

  • I signed up. How quickly can I start the program?

    Typically 24 - 48 hours. Once completing your assessment, our clinical team will enroll you in a ReachLink program and match you with your therapist. Call or request more info today!

  • How is my privacy protected?

    ReachLink is committed to keeping your privacy and confidentiality. ReachLink uses the highest standards of technology and operation management to safeguard your information. Our privacy standards are higher than required by law or regulations to ensure peace of mind.
    • Everything you tell your counselor is protected by strict federal and state laws.
    • All the messages between you and your therapist are secured and encrypted by banking-grade 256-bit encryption.
    • Our servers are protected and monitored, physically and electronically, 24 x 7 x 365.
    • Our browsing encryption system (SSL) follows modern best practices, providing world class online security and encryption.
    We need your authorization to release records to anyone. To release your records to a third party, please inform your counselor. He or she will provide you with an authorization form to complete and sign before any release of information.
