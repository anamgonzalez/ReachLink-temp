PTSD

ReachLink’s Veteran Program is designed to serve those who served. According to the U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs National Center for PTSD, trauma-focused psychotherapies such as Prolonged Exposure and Cognitive Processing Therapy are the most highly recommended types of treatment for PTSD.
ReachLink uses these innovative therapeutic techniques to deliver world-class, effective and affordable care to those who need it most.
